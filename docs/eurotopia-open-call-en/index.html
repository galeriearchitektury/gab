Open Call Eurotopia exhibition Brno

Deadline 26.02.2023

We experience a time of multi-crisis in Europe and all around the world. Advocating for political changes has never been more important. Nevertheless individual civic voices still lack proper representation on european level.

For an exhibition in architectural gallery Brno, Czech we are looking for pressing political topics by ngo`s, artists or individuals. Tell us your story accompanied with a short text and an image that will serve as an artistic representation of the project within the exhibition about political self-representation.

How will we implement your proposals?

The exhibition deals with the representational architecture of the european union and its inability to host space for the individual representation of civic voices. Thus creating conflicting situation detached from societal demand for an increased individual representation of political opinion. While the status quo will be outlined within the research part of the exhibition your submissions will be implemented in a speculative installation of self-representation.

The Installation is playing with the popular theme of the press wall. Present in images of political communication towards the public, the press wall is an omnipresent theme attached with elements of representing political actors in the context of a certain political institution.

For the Installation we will create a press wall for the speculative European Citizens Assembly. While textual Elements and the stars of the European Flag will stay present, the blue of the background will be used as a blue screen to augment the physical press wall with the images that you submit. The visitors will be able to choose the specific topic that they want to advocate for within the assembly and take a photo of themselves in front of the press wall with the certain backdrop. The visitors can then circulate in the digital public by posting them on social media. Moreover the collection will be presented on a website dedicated to the exhibition.

What do you need to send?

Send the following materials to info@galeriearchitektury.cz using cloud storage (e.g. storage/wetransfer)

  • A 3200x2200 px projected background image
  • An annotation of maximum 1000 characters
  • A list of recommended links
  • Names of authors / authors' organisations
